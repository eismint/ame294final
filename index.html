About Me

"I'm pretty simple: I like food, fun and friends."

Welcome everybody!

I've been into videogames all my life, but I've been cosplaying since 2005 and my first cosplay was Talim from Soul Calibur. Later on, I became a writer and photographer for Aftercon.com, doing features on any Anime convention that I have attended. Since then, the world of cosplay and props are stuck on me like an octopus.

I have an unhealthy relationship with anything pop culture really, especially videogames! So if you do too, let's talk over some barbecued ribs. If not, let's talk over some barbecued ribs :)
