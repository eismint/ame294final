About Me

"I'm all for adventure"

Welcome everybody!

I've been into videogames all my life, but I've been cosplaying since 2005 and my first cosplay was Talim from Soul Calibur. Later on, I became a writer and photographer for Aftercon.com, doing features on any Anime convention that I have attended. Since then, the world of cosplay and props are stuck on me like an octopus.

I have an unhealthy relationship with anything pop culture really, especially videogames!












Cosplay was a happy coincidence. I was but a meager little kid, still learning English who loved Sailor Moon that my friends and I would patrol the school playground against the evil doings of bullies. I even took up gymnastics to train up! Along with help from my loving family, I was endowed with a Sailor Jupiter costume and I became a little less meager kid who loved cosplay!

Gaming had roots which stemmed further back. My family and I strolled through Toys-R-Us and we walked out with three things: A Super Nintendo Console, Super Mario World and Mario Paint. My father sat us down with the console in hand and said, “This right here is the future.” Since then, I have come to treasure both his words and the desire to escape from (home)work.

Languages are my newest interest. Just when I was getting the hang of English, French came along with its baguettes and pungent cheeses. Then I decided to major in it and took other foreign languages for reasons none other than to travel in the future. To this day, I became intrigued with the science of languages as it literally opened up a world of possibilities!
